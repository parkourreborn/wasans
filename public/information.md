# THE WASANS SCORE INTRODUCTION
- Wasans Score is a value that determines your skill level in the game, the higher the value the better! The value of 1 means that you hold all of the world records in the game.
- Unlike other skill determination systems, we decided to determine your skill based on each wr instead of raw value, making players automatically updated with the latest route and record while also benefitting players by improving certain times into affecting their overall score
- If you are interested in applying on becoming our official member, make sure to read <#1258003982782169159> and go to <#1351374148881874944>

## How does the wasans score calculation work?
  - First, the score for each trial will be calculated by "world record time divided by your time", then add exponent by 3. [(wr/time)^3]
  - Let's say, you have 10.000 time on solar, and the world record is 9.500. 9.5 / 10 will be 0.95, applying exponent by 3 will make the value 0.875. (of course you wont be doing allat but just putting your time in wasans calculator)
  - This will be done for every single trial, outputs score for each trial. Now, average of all the trial scores will be your wasans score. Not that complicated, right?
